Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Simply download the app and sign up with your email address or phone number.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.

How do I report inappropriate content?

You can report any content by tapping the three dots menu on any video and selecting "Report".

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account anytime through the app settings or by contacting support.
