Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Unfiltered ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, profile picture, and bio
  • Content: Videos, comments, and other content you share
  • Communications: Messages and feedback you send to us

Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app and features
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Analytics: App performance and crash reports (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Enable social features like following and messaging
  • Send important notifications about your account
  • Ensure safety and prevent abuse
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Public Content: Videos and profiles you choose to make public
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our app (like cloud storage)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: View the personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Privacy Settings: Control who can see your content
  • Data Portability: Export your content and data

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: Authentication, database, and cloud storage
  • AWS S3: Video and media storage
  • Analytics Services: App performance monitoring

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Users

Our servers are located in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States, which may have different privacy laws than your country.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the effective date. Your continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
